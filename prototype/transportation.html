Transportation

Getting to Taniti and getting around the island.

Getting to Taniti

  • By Air

    Most visitors arrive by air. Taniti has a small airport that accommodates small jets and propeller planes. The airport is being expanded to welcome larger jets within the next few years.

    Flight Information / Book →

  • By Cruise Ship

    A small cruise ship docks in Yellow Leaf Bay one night per week.

    Cruise Information →

Getting Around the Island

  • Public Buses

    Serve Taniti City. Run from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. daily.

    Taniti City is fairly flat and very walkable. Merriton Landing is easy to explore on foot.
