Plan Your Trip

Frequently requested information for visitors to Taniti. This page covers: currency and payments, power outlets, alcohol regulations, language, healthcare, safety, and holidays.

  • Currency & Credit Cards
  • Power (120V)
  • Alcohol & Drinking Age
  • Language
  • Healthcare
  • Safety
  • Holidays

  • Currency

    Taniti uses the U.S. dollar. Many businesses also accept euros and yen. Several banks facilitate currency exchange. Major credit cards are widely accepted.

  • Power

    Power outlets are 120 volts (same as in the United States).

  • Alcohol

    Alcohol cannot be served or sold between midnight and 9:00 a.m. The drinking age is 18.

  • Language

    Many younger Tanitians speak fluent English. Very little English is spoken in rural areas, especially by older residents.

  • Healthcare

    One hospital and several clinics. The hospital has many multilingual employees.

  • Safety

    Violent crime is very rare. As tourism increases, there are more reports of pickpocketing and petty crimes. Take normal precautions.

  • Holidays

    Taniti has many national holidays. Tourist attractions and restaurants may be closed on holidays. Plan accordingly.
